about.

What is Hedgiehogie and what are we all about? Hedgiehogie is a museum dedicated to hedgehogs. But we're not just any ol' hedgehog museum around, we specialize in featuring the best of hedgehog goodies found all around the world in our exhibitions. Our goal isn't merely to provide you or any other visitor with some pinecone-esque things to look at, we're here to provide you with high-quality pinecone-esque things to look at! All kidding aside, the museum seeks not only to educate visitors of all ages about hedgehogs, but to hopefully win over their hearts as well. By informing the public about this often overlooked species, the museum hopes to generate more love and affection for these creatures. After all, our mission statement is: Hedgehogs taking over the world, one heart at a time.
