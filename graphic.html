  • Rhino Run

    Rhino Run Package Design

    Programs: Ai, ID

    Dimensions: 19" x 22.5"

    2012

    Rhino Run’s objective is to shed light onto the social issue of rhino poaching in South Africa through the means of a board game. By playing as a herd of rhino, I wanted to engage the player on an emotional level. The concept of Rhino Run is to get as many members of your herd to the sanctuary by avoiding danger.

  • POCO

    POCO Identity Design

    Programs: ID, Ps, Ai

    Dimensions:bucket: 8.5" x 9"

    bottle: 2.5" x 7"

    t-shirt: 20" x 28"

    shovel: 2.5" x 7.5"

    2012

    POCO is a nonprofit organization with an objective of informing people on the impact of plastic on marine life and promoting the cleanup of our beaches through the use of stationery and a promotional gift set. The concept behind the gift set is to encourage the cleanup of beaches by using the logo as a reminder of POCO’s core values and objectives.

  • Monsters

    Monsters Print Design

    Programs: ID, Ps

    Dimensions: 5.25" x 7.25"

    2012

    Monters’s objective is to illustrate the places where children expect monsters to hide. The illustrations are all original and hand drawn. This book is meant to be a fun and quick read for young adults who love pop-ups and monsters. I wanted to have suspense and the element of surprise on every spread, engaging the reader through the hand drawn illustrations and minimal text.
