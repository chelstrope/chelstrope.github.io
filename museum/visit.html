About Us:

This museums was founded in 2010, by Chelsea Strope. She loved swimming and snorkeling in the Pacific Ocean and thought she would share that love and appreciation for aquatic life by opening a museum for everyone to visit. This museum was made possible from all the help and donations of extremely generous people who also shared a love for the ocean.
